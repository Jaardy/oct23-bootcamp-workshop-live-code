### Operators

## Comparison Operators

`>` greater than,\
`<` less than,\
`>=` greater than or equal to,\
`<=` less than or equal to,\
`==` equals,\
`===` strictly equals

## Logical Operators

`&&` and,\
`||` or,\
 `!` not\
